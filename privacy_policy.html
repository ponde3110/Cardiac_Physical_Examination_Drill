1 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Cardiac Physical Examination Drill ("the App"), developed by Tatsuya Kawasaki (Matsushita Memorial Hospital), handles information in connection with your use of the App.

This App is designed exclusively for educational purposes — to help medical students, residents, and healthcare professionals learn cardiac physical examination skills. It does not provide medical advice and is not a substitute for professional clinical judgment.

2 Information We Collect

This App is designed with privacy in mind. We collect only the minimum information necessary for the App to function.

Data stored locally on your device (never transmitted):

  • Your best exam score (bestScore)
  • Meister achievement status (meisterCleared)
  • A list of questions you answered incorrectly (wrongQuestions), used for Review Mode
Important: All of the above data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (localStorage). It is never sent to any server, never shared with third parties, and never leaves your device.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

3 How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your best score on the home screen
  • Track your Meister achievement status
  • Populate Review Mode with questions you answered incorrectly

This data is never processed for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than improving your in-app learning experience.

4 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or disclose any user data to third parties. Because all data is stored locally on your device, there is no mechanism by which this App transmits information externally.

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social media plugins.

5 Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally in your browser or device's localStorage. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's local storage / app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

We retain no copies of your data on any server.

6 Patient Data and Consent

Some questions in this App include audio or video recordings of physical examination findings obtained from real patients. All such recordings were made with the informed consent of the individuals concerned. No personal identifying information (such as name, face, or date of birth) is included in any media content within the App.

7 Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by medical professionals and students aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8 Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of external breach. All data remains on your device under the protection of your operating system and browser security model.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
